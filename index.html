Operational Support

Providing aerial overwatch, terrain awareness, and situational support to assist ground teams, working under the direction of the search lead.

Workflow

Simple for producers, comfortable for crews. You tell us the shot; we handle the safe execution.

1) Pre-production

Plan & permissions

We confirm location constraints, airspace checks, crew/talent safety, and agree flight paths with the DP before cameras roll.

2) Shoot day

Repeatable execution

Clean takes, consistent framing, calm flying. We arrive early, brief the team, and prioritise predictable movement over risk.

3) Delivery

Fast handover

Footage delivered as agreed (cards, SSD, or upload). Neutral grade options available to integrate into your colour pipeline.

Search Support FAQ

Clear answers for coordinators and families — focused on safety, privacy, and trust.

Do you charge for search support?

No. Dog search and rescue drone support is always provided free of charge. This work is voluntary and values-led.

Who do you coordinate with?

We operate only in coordination with the designated search lead and with appropriate landowner permissions.

Is footage shared publicly?

No footage from active searches is shared publicly. Privacy and discretion are fundamental to how we operate.

Our Values
